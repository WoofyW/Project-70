Campus Life

School Auditorium

It was made in June 2015(when OFS's new building was made.)

This is OFS's Auditorium. This is where all the major events of OFS are held. We have had chess tornamonts in which the biggest players of chess have come and joined us. All our MUN ceremonies are also held in this auditorium. The Ciriculam Leaders also are always on our side helping us and guiding us through the right path in these big events so that they are successfull.



School Field

It was made in June 2015(when OFS's new building was made.)

This is OFS's school field. Students from a range of Grade 1 to Grade 12 play on this field. This field is used for many purposes such as the normal playing of kids at lunchtime, P.E. Lessons and P.E. Assessments. It is also used for the Interschool leagues and games in sports such as football, rugby, baseball, discus throw and javelin etc. Other Schools also come and play on this field when invited to play a friendly or competetive match againest OFS.



School Canteen

It was made in June 2015(when OFS's new building was made.)

This is OFS's canteen. This canteen is the place where all the students eat and talk with friends. All ages of students eat here and have fun. There is also a lost and found in the canteen helping students who have forgotten or lost stuff find it in the lost and found. In this canteen you get a variety of food and cusines such as Indian samosa to Italian pizza to Chinese fried rice. The kind staff members of OFS such as the teachers and the canteen staff also help you out if you have any queries.

The Chess Tournament
Came second place in National Chess Tournament

OFS's students came second in the International Chess Tournament. There were more than 62 National Inter-School Teams partoicipating in this online chess Tournament. This Tournament was organised by the Singapore Chess Federation. OFS's under-8 had gotten us the most amount of trophies and medals. 2 players from this categary also got an extra trophy because they were among the top scorers on board 1 and 3.

Swimming Competition
OFS's Swimmers parcipated in a Championship Youth Time Trial

29 of OFS's Swimmers had recently parcipated in a Singapore National Championship Youth Time Trial. Our school had exceptionaly good results with almost all of the swimmers in the top 20. The swimmers from high school managed their time very well as this was their exam period.
